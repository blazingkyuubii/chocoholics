Our History

2015 In early 2015, there were two friends who became interested in selling different kinds of merchandise but it doesn't seem to go well. Despite the concern, their passion still burns and they continue to brainstorm on what kind of things they should sell next. 2017 After a long time of discussion, the two realized that they both have a sweet tooth and have decided to sell the food that they love the most: Chocolate. Finally, in late 2017, the two started a Chocolate Shop in Quezon City to share their favorite food to others at an affordable price. 2018-2020 Their sales wasn't as good as other leading Chocolate Shops in the country, but they still kept their business as there were people who often come and buy chocolates who always served as a motivation for them to continue their business. 2020 Unfortunately, just like the other shops around them, their shop got hit with the pandemic's effect. The lockdowns forced them to shutdown their business until further notice which caused their business to crash. They had no choice but to follow the government's guidelines but that did not stop them from thinking other ways to sell chocolates. 2021-Present While creating a plan for their comeback, the two owners saw the increasing trend of online shopping which gave them an idea. They felt like the odds were at their side as they were both I.T students who has an ability to create a mobile application for their chocolate shop. Through this, the owners developed this application that serves as a Filipino online shop for chocolates. This app helped them rise again and their sales increased a month after they released their app.
